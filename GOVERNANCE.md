# Governance

Bellweather Audio Core is currently maintained by Bellweather Studios. Keith
Hetrick is the project maintainer for releases.

The project accepts issues and pull requests under the DCO policy in
`CONTRIBUTING.md`. Release timing is best-effort and follows the state of the
documented proof lanes rather than a fixed calendar.

Compatibility promises are limited to the public contracts documented in
`README.md`, `TESTING.md`, `MANIFEST.md`, and `docs/MODULES.md`.
